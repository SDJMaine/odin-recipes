Chicken Alfredo

Back to main page

Taken from allrecipes

Description

This chicken Alfredo recipe with jar sauce is a quick and easy dinner for those hectic nights. For extra flavor, cook the chicken with lemon pepper and seasoning salt.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked fettuccine or linguine
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
  • â…“ cup milk

Step-By-Step

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, place cubed cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, frozen vegetables, mushrooms, and milk in a large saucepan over medium-low heat.
  4. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve warm Alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked noodles.
